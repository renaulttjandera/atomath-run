About Us

Atomath Run is a platformer-singleplayer game that is based on mathematics and chemistry subjects. This game revolves around speedrunning to reach the end and answer the question correctly and punctually. The chemistry elements are in a form of powerups the player can acquire, such as oxygen, helium, and much more. Each element has a different effect on the player; helium gives levitation and carbon gives health.

With all that in mind, our objective is to improve the competence of SMP Santa Laurensia Alam Sutera students in mathematics and chemistry, as well increasing the competitive spirit of SMP Santa Laurensia Alam Sutera students.
